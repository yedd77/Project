Melaka River Cruise

"The River That Flows Through Time"

Take a cruise through time in Malaysia’s most historic city, Melaka. This 45-minute boat ride cruising along the vintage Melaka River moves at a peaceful pace, allowing one to admire the breathtaking view of Melaka. It’s just one of things that travellers should not miss when visiting the state. From the modern local icons to rustic colonial landmarks, here are some of the things you’ll see on the Melaka River Cruise.

Tan Kim Seng Bridge

Tribute to a wealthy Melakan trader of the same name. The bridge was destoryed in 1942 by the british and rebuilt to its current state.

Chan Koon Cheng Bridge

Replaced a drawbridge that was built in 1930 which allowed ship's passage through the Melaka River.

Pasar Bridge

Linking Kampung Hulu to the Old Central Market, this wodden bridge was built during the heydays of Portuguese rule.
